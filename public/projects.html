Microscopy

I have found what looks like metal attached to "ancient" jade from the Hongshan culture. I am not exactly sure about the implications of this because it looks like it might have been there organically and the position isn't near where you would expect metal shavings from utilizing modern tools. I have many more of these jade artifacts. They seem to be genuine because the price to material ratio is way too low to warrant any human labour and the imperfections are too obvious to indicate machining. In China it is common practice to throw away or sell those items, which can usually be found on burial grounds to circumvent the construction/private property site becoming nationalized upon discovery of historical artifacts.
